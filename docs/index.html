AU Community of Research Software Users

  • Use open source software?
  • Come to our events!
  • Learn, get support, or help others.

Community building

Meet other people interested in building new skills, sharing their knowledge, and collaborating.

Training sessions

Hands-on and practical learning on (open) software such as R or Python. We run basic to advanced workshops to expand or refine your skills.

Co-working

Share a cup of coffee, beer, or wine. Work together, chat, or get (or give) help. Brilliant ideas do not always come from staring at a laptop in solitude.

Skill sharing

Give and receive. Enjoy the range of skills that other members of the community can contribute and share your own expertise.

Resources and self-learning

Find more material to challenge your skill, keep updated, and learn even more.
